IYNA Events


As a general member of the International Youth Neuroscience Association, a wide array of events are offered for you to participate in! Our most popular international events include the IYNA Ideathon, Brain Bee Bootcamp, and Neurosurgery Series! For more events you can join our discord server to get notifications immediately on any events we have as well as opt to get email notifications through your general membership on any events. Clip on any of the images below for more information on any event you want to learn more about! 

IYNA Ideathon 2023

The IYNA Ideathon is a week-long virtual competition for high school and undergraduate students in neuroscience, where they develop research proposals to address real-world neuroscientific problems while receiving training, mentorship, and expert guidance, with winners recognized for their innovative projects.

Brain Bee Bootcamp

The Brain Bee Bootcamp, in collaboration with the International Brain Bee (IBB) and the IYNA, offers intensive preparation for Brain Bee participants worldwide, covering various aspects of the competition, including a mock IBB World Championship test and insights from previous finalists, running over 5 weekends from November 18th to December 17th.

Neurosurgery Series

Join our Neurosurgery Walk-Through Webinar on August 26th, 2023, at 11 AM and 11 PM GMT to witness live brain surgery, gain insights into surgical techniques, and engage in a Q&A session with professional neurosurgeons for an educational experience in the field of neuroscience.
